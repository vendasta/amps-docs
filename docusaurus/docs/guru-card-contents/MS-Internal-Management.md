# MS Internal Management

## Marketing Services | Adding a Salesperson to Sales & Success Center (SSC)

INTENDED FOR USE BY MANAGERS ONLY

Members of the Marketing Services Client Engagement teams are to be added as
"salespeople" to Sales & Success Center (SSC) so they can be assigned as additional
salespeople on accounts and leave notes and meeting summaries.

Adding a New Salesperson to SSC

1. Open up Partner Center in PID: VMF

2. On the left side navigation, click on Sales.

    a. Under "Management" subheading, select Salespeople:

![](/img/guru/MS-Internal-Management/01.png)

3. On the Salespeople page, click on the blue "Invite team member" button on the top right:

![](/img/guru/MS-Internal-Management/02.png)

4. You'll be brought to a page "My team" which looks very similar. Click on the "Invite team member" button here:

![](/img/guru/MS-Internal-Management/03.png)

5. A side panel will open on the right. Fill out the required fields and ensure the

permissions are as follows:

    a. First Name (full)

    b. Last Name (full)

    c. Email (Vendasta OR YDA email if no Vendasta email)

    d. Role:

    i. Admin (unchecked)

    ii. Salesperson (CHECKED)

1. Is a sales manager (unchecked)

2. Send Welcome Email to user (CHECKED, optional)

    iii. Digital Agent (unchecked)

    iv. CRM Access:

1. Can manage contacts in the platform (unchecked)

2. Can manage activities in the platform (unchecked)

3.  Can manage companies in the platform (unchecked)

![](/img/guru/MS-Internal-Management/04.png)

![](/img/guru/MS-Internal-Management/05.png)

6. Click Send. The user is now a salesperson in SSC.
