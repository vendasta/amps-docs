## PRIORITY 3 (MINOR)

* Description: Small errors made in basic support functions that have caused frustration to partner

/client.

* Handled By: Client Solution Specialist.

* Actions:

* Provide basic support and attempt initial troubleshooting.

* Record the issue for tracking purposes.

* Impact: No immediate risk to SMB/partner; escalation or missed resolution will not affect SMB

/partner significantly.

## PRIORITY 2 (MAJOR)

* Description: Intermediate support for more complex problems that require more technical

knowledge.

* Handled By: Senior Manager of the cohort.

* Actions:

* Apply intermediate support and technical troubleshooting.

* Record the issue due to potential revenue at risk.

* Impact: SMB is at risk; timely and effective resolution is crucial to mitigate revenue loss.

## PRIORITY 1 (CRITICAL)

Escalation Tiers Defined;

* Description: Advanced support for the most complex and critical issues requiring deep technical

expertise.

* Handled By: Director and GM of Marketing Services.

* Actions:

* Implement advanced troubleshooting and high level strategic solutions.

* Record the issue due to the partnership and multiple SMBs being at risk.

* Impact: Partner and multiple SMBs are at risk; immediate and thorough resolution is essential to

protect partnerships and prevent widespread impact.
