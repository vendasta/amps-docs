# Inbox Pro Enablement Process

Inbox Pro is an AI used on a website to help gather leads (potential customers) through features like 
chat, text messages, and Google Business Messages. All the communication is organized in one 
place for the team to see, and a helpful chat assistant ensures businesses take advantage of potential 
customers visiting their websites.
