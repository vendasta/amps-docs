# Google Adwords How to check Search Ads quality score and it's importance

## STEP BY STEP INSTRUCTIONS TO CHECK QUALITY SCORE:

Access Google Ads:

* Go to 
 and log in with your Google account.
Google Ads

Navigate to Keywords:

* In the left hand menu, click on "Campaigns."

* Select the campaign where your keywords are located.

View Quality Score:

* Click on the "Keywords" tab.

* The Quality Score column might not be visible by default. If not, click on the columns icon

above the performance summary graph and select "Modify columns."

* Look for "Quality Score" in the available columns and add it to your selected columns.

* Click "Apply" to save your changes.

Review Quality Score:

* You should now see the Quality Score for each keyword in the "Keywords" tab.

Quality Score is typically represented on a scale of 1 to 10, with 10 being the highest. The score is 
based on factors such as keyword relevance, ad text quality, and landing page experience.

## WHY QUALITY SCORE IS IMPORTANT FOR A SEARCH CAMPAIGN:

Ad Position and Visibility:

* Higher Quality Scores can result in better ad positions. Your ads are more likely to appear

in top positions on the search results page, increasing visibility.

Cost Per Click (CPC):

* A higher Quality Score can lead to lower costs. Google rewards advertisers with relevant

and high quality ads by offering lower costs per click (CPC). This means you can

potentially pay less for each click.

Ad Rank:

* Ad Rank is a crucial factor in determining where your ad appears on the search results

page. Quality Score is a significant component of the Ad Rank formula. A higher Quality

Score contributes to a better Ad Rank.

Click Through Rate (CTR):

* Quality Score takes into account the historical performance of your keywords, including

the click through rate (CTR). Improving the relevance of your ads can lead to a higher CTR,

which positively impacts Quality Score.

Campaign Optimization:

* Monitoring and improving your Quality Score encourages continuous optimization of your

campaigns. It prompts you to create more relevant ads, choose appropriate keywords, and

enhance the user experience on your landing pages.

Understanding and improving Quality Score is crucial for running successful and cost effective search 
campaigns on Google Ads.
