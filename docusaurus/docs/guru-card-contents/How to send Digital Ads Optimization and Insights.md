# How to send Digital Ads Optimization and Insights

Go to VMKA, paste the AGID or business name search for the Optimization pdf. Refer example below.

![](/img/guru/How%20to%20send%20Digital%20Ads%20Optimization%20and%20Insights/01.png)

After finding the PDF go to Zendesk, select macro as below.

![](/img/guru/How%20to%20send%20Digital%20Ads%20Optimization%20and%20Insights/02.png)

Draft the email accordingly and update the ticket number in the VMKA task where your name is 
reflected.

![](/img/guru/How%20to%20send%20Digital%20Ads%20Optimization%20and%20Insights/03.png)
