Standard Detailed Breakdown:

Phase 1: Information Gathering

Order is submitted and processed, ensuring all required questions in the fulfillment form

are filled out.

A confirmation email is sent out in Zendesk. (using the Email Marketing   ORDER

CONFIRMATION macro)

Requesting to set up an onboarding call if assets are outstanding, the onboarding

call is recorded and added to the VTM task notes

Project is created in Vendasta Task Manager:

Assign Client Engagement/Comms tasks to Respective Client Engagement Client

Solutions Specialist.

IF ONBOARDING CALL IS REQUIRED: The onboarding call is completed and the Client

Solution Specialists verify the information provided on the fulfillment form & ask clarifying

questions to close any gaps.

Customer list

Domain access (or confirm if the client will be able to edit DNS records themself)

IF ONBOARDING CALL IS REQUIRED: Send post onboarding email to the client/partner. (

using the Email Marketing   ORDER CONFIRMATION macro)

Email 
 with any outstanding inform + onboarding notes (if 
content@yourdigitalagents.com

applicable).

If we had all information necessary to start from the initial order, let the team know

we are good to proceed.

## ONBOARDING IS COMPLETE AND THE INITIAL TICKET CAN BE SOLVED UNLESS FOLLOWING UP
FOR OUTSTANDING ASSETS.

Phase 2: Delivery of Quarterly Strategy & Email Content (recurring)

Content team will draft content, email strategy and content schedule which will be uploaded in

Task Manager for CE to send out (
)
Send Email Content for Approval Task in VTM

:::tip
Note: Email Marketing campaign will not be launched without client/partner approval
:::

(no approval is required for the schedule/strategy)

Upon delivery of the first strategy, the client will be required to turn on triggers in Business

App. Due to security concerns, superadmins are not able to complete this action.
