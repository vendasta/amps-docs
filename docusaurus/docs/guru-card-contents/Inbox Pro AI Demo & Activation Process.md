Process for Training the AI

"Prior to training the AI, I want to showcase what it knows"

Ask a question based on information on the home page of the website.

Go to another page and ask a question.

"The AI is going to do it's best to gather your name, phone number, and email, and it's actually going 
to add it right to the CRM for you, which I'll take you through later. Before we move on, important to 
mention that the AI has upwards of 150 languages, so if someone doesn't know what it's saying they 
can ask it to switch to their language. Moving on, now we are going to Train your AI."

Navigate back to Business App for the business &gt; Under Knowledge Sources &gt; +Add new knowledge

![](/img/guru/Inbox%20Pro%20AI%20Demo%20%26%20Activation%20Process/07.png)

You can add a little of both Text and Website training for the AI, start with the website:

Website &gt; Grab URL from the home page &gt; Name of it &gt; Follow Links (it'll scrape every link up to 100) 
&gt; Save &gt; It'll show the pages found &gt; Next

Text &gt; Ask business owner, "Whats one thing that wouldn't be on your website but someone normally 
asks"

Put Q: fill in the question

A: add the answer

Add a title for what the question is.

SAVE

Reload the website and test the new knowledge.
