# When to reach out to a Digital Strategist 

As a Client Solution Specialist, you should:

* be able to find the information you need in both AdIntell and MatchCraft

* be able to look at our analytics and interpret what they are showing

* Be able to answer basic questions about the analytics

* What is going good/ bad?

* Why is this metric important?

* What does each metric mean?

The DA Strategists will help with:

* Anything that has to do with performance

* Provide guidance, and recommendations for future performance (always looking ahead)

* Answer questions like:

* What should we do going forward?

* How is my campaign performing?

* What can we do differently to

* improve performance?
