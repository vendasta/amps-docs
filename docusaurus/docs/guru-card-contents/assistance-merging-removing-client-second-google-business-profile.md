# Assistance with Merging or Removing a Client's Second Google Business Profile

To merge both profiles or remove the unwanted profile so that the client can keep all the reviews on
both listings.

We can assist with the process of merging or removing the additional Google Business Profile (GBP).

:::tip
Note: No additional product activation is required for this service. if the client already has GBP
optimization active.
:::

Steps:

Ensure we have access to both listings and both listings are verified.

Reopen the old GBP optimization project and inform the team.
