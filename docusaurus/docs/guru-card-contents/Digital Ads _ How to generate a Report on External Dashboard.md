# Digital Ads How to generate a Report on External Dashboard

Go to Partner Centre and go to Ad Intel

![](/img/guru/Digital%20Ads%20_%20How%20to%20generate%20a%20Report%20on%20External%20Dashboard/01.png)

on the left hand menu click 'External Dashboards' and click on the campaign you want to work on

![](/img/guru/Digital%20Ads%20_%20How%20to%20generate%20a%20Report%20on%20External%20Dashboard/02.png)

Again on the left hand menu click 'Export'

![](/img/guru/Digital%20Ads%20_%20How%20to%20generate%20a%20Report%20on%20External%20Dashboard/03.png)

Choose the date range you want to have in the report and customize the data to your needs.

![](/img/guru/Digital%20Ads%20_%20How%20to%20generate%20a%20Report%20on%20External%20Dashboard/04.png)

Click 'Create PDF' when complete

The document will be saved to your download folder.

Note! The report is not white labeled and 
will contain the cost of the campaign
 to the partner. Ensure

this is clear when sending over to the partner.
