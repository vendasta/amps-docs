# Social Marketing Platform POST FAILING

If you are facing any failed post and the Content team is reaching out to you.

When you check the Connect accounts place everything will be normal.

![](/img/guru/Social%20Marketing%20Platform%20-%20POST%20FAILING/01.png)

But even though the posts are failing.

![](/img/guru/Social%20Marketing%20Platform%20-%20POST%20FAILING/02.png)

Just see what platform is failing and just try reconnecting it again by clicking the three dot button and 
Edit Connection

![](/img/guru/Social%20Marketing%20Platform%20-%20POST%20FAILING/03.png)

Then it will ask for the permission. Click on reconnect and then it will ask the name of the account you 
want to connect click that account name and it will get connected

![](/img/guru/Social%20Marketing%20Platform%20-%20POST%20FAILING/04.png)

![](/img/guru/Social%20Marketing%20Platform%20-%20POST%20FAILING/05.png)

After that it will get connected again and now if you check the failed post and repost it. It will get 
successfully posted.

![](/img/guru/Social%20Marketing%20Platform%20-%20POST%20FAILING/06.png)
