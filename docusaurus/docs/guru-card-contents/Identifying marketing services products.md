# Identifying marketing services products

We all know that one way to identify the marketing services product is through the dark border line 
around the product logo. But there is another way to filter all the marketing services products and it's 
through the partner center. Please look at the steps below:

Step 1: Go to partner center: 
https://partners.vendasta.com/dashboard?marketId=default

Step 2: Click on Marketplace &gt; Discover products

Step 3: Click on "Filters"&gt; Add filter &gt; Vendor &gt; Marketing services and then click on "Apply". All the 
marketing services products along with their prices will show up.
