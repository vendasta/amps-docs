# Why doesn't my listing show on Google 

In the digital age, having a strong online presence is crucial for businesses of all sizes. One of the 
primary avenues for potential customers to discover your business is through Google searches. 
However, what happens when your listing doesn't show up as expected?

A recurring question is the visibility of a business listing on Google. When the user searched for "The 
Eli Winter Springs Apartments," the desired listing appeared promptly. However, upon removing 
"Apartments" from the search query, another business popped up instead.

This scenario is not uncommon and highlights the importance of optimizing your business listing to 
improve visibility. There are many aspects that impact which business lands in that number on spot on 
the local map pack for local searches   
Local Search Ranking Factors

While we cannot remove the other business from the map, we can work on improving some aspects 
of Eli Winter Spring's performance to increase their rank.

Such as:

* Increasing number of reviews & responses

* Paid SEO Program

* Verifying all listing information is syncing correctly

* Updating Google Business Profile as much as possible with all relevant fields

:::tip
Note: There is also a portion of local search ranking that is based on the IP address/characteristics of 
the person searching
:::
