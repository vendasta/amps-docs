# What are Google's Guidelines for GBP listings

https://support.google.com/business/answer/3038177?hl=en#zippy=%2Cname%2Ci want to activate 
my suspended buisness account

To be able to qualify for a Google listing, the Business needs to

* Have a physical location that customers can visit

OR

* The business travels to the customer's location to provide the service.

In both cases, the business needs to meet the clients face to face.

There are a variety of requirements; one of the most recurring is how the business name is displayed. 
Here are the guidelines for this.
