# How to find a missing Social Website Content & Creation Form

Go to Partner Centre and change the PID to VWCT.

Under Business&gt; Accounts search for the relevant AGID

Website AG JLJCV7WNK5

Social AG 334XVCX72C

Go to Website Pro and click Website Pro Dashboard and click Forms (See below)

![](/img/guru/How%20to%20find%20a%20missing%20Social_Website%20Content%20%26%20Creation%20Form/01.png)

Click on Entries in the 'Website Creation and Content Form' section

From the below view, you can see if the form was started and the progress status.

![](/img/guru/How%20to%20find%20a%20missing%20Social_Website%20Content%20%26%20Creation%20Form/02.png)

Click View by hovering under the Business name column and you can view or download the PDF to 
show to the client or partner.

![](/img/guru/How%20to%20find%20a%20missing%20Social_Website%20Content%20%26%20Creation%20Form/03.png)
