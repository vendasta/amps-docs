# Google Ad Grant Notable Limitations 

Google Ad Grant can only be use for search/text ads.

Budget Constraints:
$10 daily limit per campaign
Challenging in competitive markets

Bid Restrictions:
Maximum cost per click (CPC) limits ($2)
Lower ad positions in competitive niches

Ad Positioning:
Lower visibility due to budget and bid limitations
Impact on click through rate (CTR)

Ad Eligibility:
Restrictions on ad types and extensions
Limits advertising options

Strict Compliance:
Need to adhere to Google's guidelines
Risk of grant suspension for non compliance

Geographic Targeting Challenges:
Complexity in reaching local audiences (these are usually small audience sizes) 
Especially if operating in multiple regions

Campaign Complexity:
Managing multiple campaigns with unique elements
Requires expertise in digital marketing

Conversion Tracking:
Limited infrastructure for effective tracking
Challenges in measuring campaign success

Competitive Niches:
Di?culty outperforming competitors with larger budgets
Especially in fundraising or health related causes

Limited Reporting:
Restricted reporting features compared to paid accounts
Limited insights into campaign performance

Policy Changes:
Google's policy updates may require campaign adjustments
Staying informed is crucial

Limited Support:
Minimal customer support for Ad Grants
