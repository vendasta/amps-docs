# How to find a Bing listing

If the partner/client is asking to see the Bing listing that we create after working on their Google 
listing, here's what you have to do:

Go to 
Bing.com

Search for the business using name and address (to ensure you get the right one)

![](/img/guru/How%20to%20find%20a%20Bing%20listing/01.png)

Press the arrow on the top corner of the listing

![](/img/guru/How%20to%20find%20a%20Bing%20listing/02.png)

which will take you to this screen

![](/img/guru/How%20to%20find%20a%20Bing%20listing/03.png)

Click the share button and copy the URL ready for sharing
