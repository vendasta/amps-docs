# New Page Experience How to accept page access request

First be logged in Facebook on a desktop or laptop computer. You cannot accept page access

from a mobile device.

Switch to your Business Facebook page   so that you are interacting as your Business page  

this can be done at by clicking at the very top   and switching profile.

![](/img/guru/New%20Page%20Experience%20-%20How%20to%20accept%20page%20access%20request/01.png)

When you are interacting as your page then on your left hand side you see a menu like below

![](/img/guru/New%20Page%20Experience%20-%20How%20to%20accept%20page%20access%20request/02.png)

  Now click on the 'Settings' which will take you to this Tab

![](/img/guru/New%20Page%20Experience%20-%20How%20to%20accept%20page%20access%20request/03.png)

From here you will click 'New Page Experience' which will take to this tab

![](/img/guru/New%20Page%20Experience%20-%20How%20to%20accept%20page%20access%20request/04.png)

Here under partner with access you will find a Request from Digital Marketing services

![](/img/guru/New%20Page%20Experience%20-%20How%20to%20accept%20page%20access%20request/05.png)

All you have to do is review the request   approve it by entering your Facebook password

One thing to be aware of   if you are not the owner of the Facebook page you won't be able to

view the request.

After accepting the request we will have access to the Facebook page
