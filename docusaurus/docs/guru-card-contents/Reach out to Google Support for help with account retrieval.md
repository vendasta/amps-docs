# Reach out to Google Support for help with account retrieval

https://myaccount.google.com/support?pli=1

Scroll to the bottom of the page to find the below

![](/img/guru/Reach%20out%20to%20Google%20Support%20for%20help%20with%20account%20retrieval/01.png)

Click Report an issue

![](/img/guru/Reach%20out%20to%20Google%20Support%20for%20help%20with%20account%20retrieval/02.png)

![](/img/guru/Reach%20out%20to%20Google%20Support%20for%20help%20with%20account%20retrieval/03.png)

Choose recover account/password

![](/img/guru/Reach%20out%20to%20Google%20Support%20for%20help%20with%20account%20retrieval/04.png)

give a detailed explanation with the blocker you are experiencing with logging into the account
