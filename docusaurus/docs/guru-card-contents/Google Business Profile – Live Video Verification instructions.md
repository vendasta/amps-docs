Purpose: Guide to successfully completing the live video verification required by Google to verify your 
business

## PREPARING FOR THE LIVE VIDEO CALL

* Ensure you have a strong and stable internet connection.

* Use a laptop or desktop computer for better video quality.

* Choose a quiet, well lit space with no distractions.

* Have the following documents ready:

* Business license

* Utility bills

* Lease agreements (must show business name and address)

## HOW TO ACCESS THE VERIFICATION CALL

Log in to your Gmail account

Go to Google My Business

Enter your business name and click "Get Verified"

Select the "Live Video Verification" option

Follow the instructions on screen to proceed

## DURING THE LIVE VIDEO CALL

* Allow access to your camera and microphone when prompted.

* Carefully follow the instructions from the Google representative.

* Slowly pan your camera to show:

* Exterior signage and building

* Business operations (products, services, equipment)

* Proof of access (keys, restricted areas)

* Supporting documents with business name and address

* Be prepared to answer questions about your business.

* Do not end the call until the representative confirms all requirements are met.

## TIPS FOR A SUCCESSFUL CALL

* Practice your walkthrough beforehand to ensure a smooth flow.

* Speak clearly and describe what you're showing on camera.

* If verifying multiple locations, be prepared to repeat the process for each one.

* Make sure your business information (name, address, hours) is consistent across platforms.

* Remain patient and cooperative throughout the process.

Following these steps will significantly improve your chances of a successful verification with Google.
