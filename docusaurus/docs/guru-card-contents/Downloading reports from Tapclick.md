# Downloading reports from Tapclick

After you have navigated through TapClicks and now you have to download the report, please follow 
the below steps. Before jumping on that, if you would like more information on reaching this point, 
please use the below link for a Guru card regarding that.

https://app.getguru.com/card/ijaqjRgT/Navigating through tapclicks

Step 1: So if you would like to download the excel directly, you can click on this download button on 
the top right (s marked on the screenshot)

![](/img/guru/Downloading%20reports%20from%20Tapclick/01.png)

Step 2: You can also have the report customized and for that, you can click on the three dots on the 
top right corner.

![](/img/guru/Downloading%20reports%20from%20Tapclick/02.png)

Step 3: Click on "Build PDF report"

![](/img/guru/Downloading%20reports%20from%20Tapclick/03.png)

Step 4: You can select options from the list or select the default cover.

![](/img/guru/Downloading%20reports%20from%20Tapclick/04.png)

Step 5: Then, you can download it!

![](/img/guru/Downloading%20reports%20from%20Tapclick/05.png)
