## DRAWBACKS OF INSTANT EXPERIENCE ADS

Mobile Only Placement: Limits exposure to desktop users.

Creative Asset Requirements: Demands high quality visuals and videos.

Niche Targeting: May not perform optimally for audiences relying on desktop.
