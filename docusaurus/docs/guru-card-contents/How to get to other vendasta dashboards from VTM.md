# How to get to other vendasta dashboards from VTM

Find the account you want to use

Click products and services

![](/img/guru/How%20to%20get%20to%20other%20vendasta%20dashboards%20from%20VTM/01.png)

This will show everything they have active

![](/img/guru/How%20to%20get%20to%20other%20vendasta%20dashboards%20from%20VTM/02.png)

From here you can click on any of the dashboards and be redirected to them
